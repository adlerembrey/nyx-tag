Privacy Policy for Nyx Tag

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Nyx Tag. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and your rights when using Nyx Tag.

Information We Collect

Nyx Tag may collect or store the following information:

  • PlayFab Player ID
  • Display name
  • Competitive statistics, such as MMR, rank, and progression
  • Cosmetic ownership and in-game currency balances
  • Discord User ID and Discord username only if you choose to link your Discord account
  • Gameplay analytics used to improve the game

Nyx Tag does not collect passwords, payment card information, or personal identification documents.

Voice Chat

Nyx Tag uses your device microphone for real-time multiplayer voice communication during gameplay. Voice communications are not recorded or stored by Nyx Tag.

Discord Account Linking

If you choose to link your Discord account, your Discord User ID and Discord username may be stored to verify account ownership, grant Discord-exclusive rewards, synchronize Discord roles, and provide community features. You may unlink your Discord account using the Discord bot command or supported in-game features.

Third-Party Services

Nyx Tag uses third-party services to provide multiplayer, account, community, and platform features. These may include:

  • PlayFab / Microsoft
  • Photon Engine
  • Discord
  • Meta Horizon Platform

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to save player progression, maintain competitive rankings, synchronize cosmetic ownership, support Discord account linking, improve gameplay, detect abuse or cheating, and provide player support.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect player information using secure services provided by PlayFab, Meta, and other platform providers.

Children's Privacy

Nyx Tag is intended for users who meet the minimum age requirements established by Meta for the Meta Horizon platform. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children outside of information required to provide gameplay services.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Nyx Tag after an update means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For support, questions, or privacy requests, contact us through the official Nyx Tag Discord server or support page.
